INGREDIENTS

  • One whole salad
  • Vinegar

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

STEPS

  1. Clean the salad
  2. Put it all in a salad bowl
  3. Cut garlic into tiny pieces
  4. Add oil, salt and vinegar
  5. Stir
